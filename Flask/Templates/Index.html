Brick & Mortar vs. ECommerce

Big Box Stores Pivoting to Stay Relevant

Is live shopping going to be a thing of the past?

Walmart leads retail giants as they expand their scope to strategically
initiate a market for sellers such as that of their direct competition, Amazon.
The goal of our Analysis thru Machine Learning is to take the leading retail giant's
public data (the very little amount there is) and use that to determine if Brick and Mortar
is suffering due to ECommerce sales. To accomplish this we are utilizing Random Forest, Linear Regression and
Logistic Regression Models, predicting future sales and comparing.
